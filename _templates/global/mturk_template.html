This HIT is an experimental study conducted by Justus-Liebig-University Giessen.
It contains three parts: 1) playing a lottery 2) working on tasks concerning decision-making 3) answering some concluding questions.

Please schedule around 40 minutes for completing this HIT.
The compensation amounts to $X on average ($Y fix payment for completing the HIT + $X variable payment on average based on your decision during the HIT).

Requirements to succesfully complete the HIT:

- You should be familiar with the concept of Expected Values.
- If it becomes apparent that you work inattentivly on this HIT (e.g., skipping instructional pages), you will not be able to complete the HIT and thus won't receive any compensation.
- So please make sure that you focus on this HIT and complete it without any interruptions.
- Mobile devices are not allowed!


Briefly after completing this HIT, you will receive your compensation.
