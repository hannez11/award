Failure Award - fail smart!

CleverClean Inc. is one of the first companies that implemented a new type of reward for its managers - the Failure Award. A newspaper recently published the following article about CleverClean’s unique usage of Failure Awards:

During the reward ceremony every manager who received a Failure Award is applauded by his or her colleagues and superiors. Further, the management board organizes a party for the whole department and the winner is toasted to with Champaign and delicious food.

The two most recent Failure Award winners are described in the following:



In 2020 Max received a Failure Award for his innovative project of creating a cleaning product for universal usage. Unfortunately, after starting the project it turned out that the overall product won’t be profitable. Max received the Failure Award for his courage to start a risky and innovative project even though it ended up failing and had to be terminated!

In 2019 Savannah received a Failure Award for her project “carefree clean”, which she had to end shortly after starting the project due to technical issues. However, the project itself was unique and could have been a successful breakthrough. Savannah received the Failure Award for trying something new although the project ended up failing.

You could be the next person who receives the Failure Award!

These are the general requirements to receive a Failure Award:

CleverClean Inc. grants Failure Awards to managers who have the courage to take value adding risks by trying something new and are not afraid to fail big.

How do you, as a leading project manager, qualify to receive a Failure Award?

In case you take value adding risks but your project ends up failing, you can be certain to receive a Failure Award if you deliberately decide for project discontinuation. If you decide to discontinue your project at a later point of time, you still have the chance to earn a Failure Award. However, in case of delayed project discontinuation, a Failure Award is only granted with a 50% chance.

How does CleverClean Inc. define a failing project?

A project is defined as failing if investing a dollar in this project creates lower expected returns than investing in alternative projects.

Besides the reward ceremony, what exactly do you receive as a Failure Award?

CleverClean Inc. has allocated a total budget of 0.2 million Lira for its Failure Awards. Each Failure Award is worth 1% of the 0.2 million Lira budget.
