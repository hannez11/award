Courage Award

CleverClean Inc. is one of the first companies that implemented a new type of reward for its managers - the Courage Award. A newspaper recently published the following article about CleverClean’s unique usage of Courage Awards:

During the reward ceremony every manager who received a Courage Award is applauded by his or her colleagues and superiors. Further, the management board organizes a party for the whole department and the winner is toasted to with Champaign and delicious food.

The two most recent Courage Award winners are described in the following:



In 2020 Max received a Courage Award for his innovative project of creating a cleaning product for universal usage. Unfortunately, after starting the project it turned out that the overall product won’t be profitable. Max received the Courage Award for his courage to start a risky and innovative project even though it ended up failing and had to be terminated!

In 2019 Savannah received a Courage Award for her project “carefree clean”, which she had to end shortly after starting the project due to technical issues. However, the project itself was unique and could have been a successful breakthrough. Savannah received the Courage Award for trying something new although the project ended up failing.
