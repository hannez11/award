The experiment consists of three parts. Please read the following procedure carefully.


  • Part 1: Lottery-Task
    First, you will receive a lottery task. Its payout is an additional part of your compensation. The lottery is independent from this HIT’s main-task. You will find detailed instructions on the next page.

  • Part 2: Main-Task
    In the main task you are taking on the role of a project manager at the company CleverClean Inc. During the experiment you will receive several sets of information and will be asked to make various decisions.

  • Part 3: Questionnaire
    Lastly, you will be asked to answer some questions about your made decisions and your person at the end of the experiment. There are no right or wrong answers. Therefore, please be honest and read them carefully. Your answers have no influence on your compensation. All data will be treated anonymously by the experiment administrator.
