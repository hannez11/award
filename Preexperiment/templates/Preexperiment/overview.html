The experiment consists of three parts. Please read the following procedure carefully.


  • Part 1: Lottery-Task
    First, you will receive a lottery task. Its payout is one part of your compensation. The lottery is independent from this HIT’s main-task. You will find detailed instructions on the next page.

  • Part 2: Main-Task
    In the main task you are taking on the role of a project manager at the company CleverClean Inc. During the experiment you will receive several sets of information and will be asked to make various decisions.

  • Part 3: Questionnaire
    Lastly, you will be asked to answer some questions about your decisions and your person at the end of the experiment.
