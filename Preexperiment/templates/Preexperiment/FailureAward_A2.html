Failure Award - fail smart!

CleverClean Inc. is one of the first companies that implemented a new type of reward for its managers - the Failure Award. A newspaper recently published the following article about CleverClean’s unique usage of Failure Awards:

During the reward ceremony every manager who received a Failure Award is applauded by his or her colleagues and superiors. Further, the management board organizes a party for the whole department and the winner is toasted to with Champaign and delicious food.

The two most recent Failure Award winners are described in the following:



In 2020 Max received a Failure Award for his project of creating a cleaning product for universal usage. Unfortunately, after starting the project it turned out that the overall product won’t be profitable. Because Max quickly decided to terminate the failing project and thus prevented that more money is wasted in the project, he received the Failure Award.

In 2019 Savannah received a Failure Award for her project “carefree clean”, which she deliberately ended shortly after starting the project due to technical issues. However, the project itself was unique and could have been a successful breakthrough. Savannah took the right decision by terminating it as soon as evidence was given that the project is failing, that is why she received the Failure Award.
